Teaching Philosophy

In my teaching, I emphasize statistics and econometrics as tools for thinking about uncertainty and for answering real-world policy questions. I aim to build students’ confidence with mathematical ideas, provide hands-on experience with data (using software such as R and Stata), and create an inclusive learning environment where all students feel comfortable asking questions and making mistakes.
