1. Job Market Paper:
The Effects of Extended Medicaid Postpartum Coverage on Insurance and Mental Health Outcomes.
With Sankar Mukhopadhyay.

This paper evaluates the impact of recent postpartum Medicaid coverage extensions on insurance coverage, mental health measures, and related outcomes using a staggered difference-in-differences framework and data from the Household Pulse Survey.
